Stroke Symptoms - FAST


- In order to help someone having the Symptoms of stroke, one needs to be aware of FAST.

- FAST is an acronym to help detect and enhance responsiveness to the needs of a person having a stroke.

- The acronym stands for Facial drooping, Arm weakness, Speech difficulties and Time to call emergency services.
